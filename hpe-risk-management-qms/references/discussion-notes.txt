# Risk #1 Discussion Notes – BCP: Business Continuity

## 1. Announcement Protocol — Mitigation or Contingency?

| Jenis       | Definisi                                 | Contoh Risk #1                                                  |
|-------------|------------------------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------|
| Mitigation  | Proactive — siapkan SEBELUM risk terjadi | Buat SOP Komunikasi BCP — define who, channel, timing, template |
| Contingency | Reactive — jalankan SETELAH risk terjadi | Eksekusi SOP Komunikasi — kirimkan announcement ke customer     |

Jawab: Pendefinisian protokol (siapa, channel apa, template) masuk Mitigation. Eksekusi announcement saat incident terjadi masuk Contingency.

Tapi dalam Risk Register, cukup tulis "BCP Communication Protocol" sebagai satu item di Contingency, karena "siapa & channel apa" akan terdokumentasi di SOP terpisah.

## 2. Siapa yang Announce? — High Management vs Manager

| Customer Tier | Contoh | Announcement By | Channel | Timing |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Tier 1 (Strategic) | Bank Danamon, Mandiri | Service Delivery Manager — untuk operational update | Telepon langsung + Email formal | Dalam 1 jam |
| Tier 1 — Escalation | Jika ada SLA breach / kontraktual | Top Management (Ops Director/ASM) untuk apology & account-level communication | Telepon + Email | Dalam 2 jam |
| Tier 2 (Standard) | Non‑critical accounts | Service Delivery Manager | Email blast | Dalam 2 jam |
| Tier 3 (Ad‑hoc) | Non‑contract customer | Dispatcher/CS | Email | Dalam 4 jam |

**Kenapa ini pendekatan yang baik?**
- Tidak semua escalation perlu ke top management — membingungkan dan tidak efisien
- Tapi untuk customer kunci, top management involvement menunjukkan commitment & accountability
- Auditor suka pendekatan risk‑based seperti ini — proportional terhadap importance

## 3. Revisi CSR — Golden Unit Logic

### Perbandingan: Sebelum vs Sesudah

| Kondisi                     | Before                                  | After                                                    |
|-----------------------------|-----------------------------------------|----------------------------------------------------------|
| Customer dengan Golden Unit | CSR tanpa klarifikasi                   | ✅ CSR via remote guidance — spare part sudah di site    |
| Customer tanpa Golden Unit  | CSR — impossible                        | ✅ Schedule support + estimate waktu transportasi normal |
| Transportasi terganggu      | Kontradiksi (CSR butuh kirim spare)     | ✅ Resolved — Golden Unit bypass kebutuhan transport     |
| Consistency                 | Satu plan untuk semua — tidak realistis | ✅ Tiered approach — sesuai kemampuan customer           |

### Contoh Isi Setelah Direvisi

| Section | Isi Baru |
|---|---|
| **Mitigation Plan** | • Maintain database emergency contact customer (runs already)<br>• Define BCP Communication Protocol — who, channel, timing, template per customer tier<br>• Maintain Golden Unit customer list & ensure currency |
| **Contingency Plan** | • For customers with Golden Unit: Activate CSR via remote guidance (phone/email). No transportation dependency.<br>• For customers without Golden Unit: Reschedule support. Provide ETA based on transportation recovery estimate. Proactive communication via defined protocol.<br>• Execute BCP Communication Protocol: SDM call Tier 1 customers, email blast others.<br>• Escalate to Top Management if SLA breach incurred on Tier 1 accounts. |

## 4. Prediksi Pertanyaan Auditor — & Jawaban

| Pertanyaan | Jawabanmu Bisa |
|---|---|
| "You mentioned CSR for Golden Unit customers. Do you have an updated list of which customers have Golden Units — and which don't?" | "Yes, it's part of our Mitigation Plan — we will maintain this list and review quarterly." |
| "How do you estimate 'when transportation will return to normal'?" | "We have MoU with alternative logistics vendors. ETA is based on their real‑time assessment, plus historical data on route recovery times." |
| "Who in your team has the authority to activate the BCP Communication Protocol?" | "The Service Delivery Manager on duty. For Tier 1 escalation, the Ops Director/ASM is notified within 2 hours." |
| "This plan differentiates by customer tier. How do you ensure it's applied consistently?" | "The BCP Communication Protocol SOP will define decision tree by customer tier. Training will be done annually." |
